Technical SEO Audit

Technical SEO forms the bedrock of your website's search engine visibility. It ensures that crawlers can efficiently discover, understand, and index your content. A strong technical foundation is crucial for any successful SEO strategy.

Crawlability & Indexing

+

Our checks confirm `mandbhealth.com` is generally crawlable and indexable. Google Search Console indicates most pages are indexed, which is a positive sign. However, we've identified a few areas for optimization:

  • Ensure `robots.txt` is not inadvertently blocking important sections.
  • Verify `sitemap.xml` is up-to-date and submitted to Google Search Console.
  • Monitor GSC's "Index Coverage" report for any "Excluded" or "Error" pages.
  • Implement proper canonical tags on pages with similar content to prevent duplicate content issues.

SSL Certificate & Security

+

`mandbhealth.com` currently uses HTTPS with a valid SSL certificate, which is excellent for security and a positive ranking signal.

  • Ensure the certificate remains valid and is renewed before expiration.
  • Verify all internal links and resources are loaded via HTTPS to avoid mixed content warnings.

Mobile Responsiveness

+

The website appears generally responsive across various devices. With Google's mobile-first indexing, this is crucial.

  • Conduct thorough testing on a wider range of mobile devices and screen sizes.
  • Pay attention to touch target sizes and font readability on smaller screens.
  • Ensure no content is hidden or difficult to access on mobile.

Site Structure & Usability (UX)

An intuitive site structure and excellent user experience are vital for both search engine crawlers and human visitors. They help users find information easily and signal positive engagement to search engines.

  • Content Organization: Review how services, conditions, and blog posts are categorized. Ensure a logical hierarchy (e.g., `/services/therapy/` or `/conditions/depression/`).
  • Navigation Menu: The current navigation is clear. Ensure all key services and information are easily accessible within 2-3 clicks from the homepage.
  • URL Structure: Optimize URLs to be clean, descriptive, and keyword-rich (e.g., `mandbhealth.com/services/spravaro-treatment` instead of `mandbhealth.com/page-id=123`).
  • Internal Linking: Strategically link related pages (e.g., from a blog post about depression to the SPRAVATO treatment page) to distribute "link equity" and aid user navigation.
  • User Testing: Consider conducting small-scale user tests to identify any hidden navigation or usability pain points.

Content & On-Page SEO

High-quality, relevant content is the cornerstone of effective SEO, especially for a healthcare website where E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) is paramount.

Content Quality & E-E-A-T

  • Expertise: Ensure all medical content is written or reviewed by qualified healthcare professionals. Clearly display credentials.
  • Authoritativeness: Cite reputable sources for medical claims. Link to relevant studies or official health organizations.
  • Trustworthiness: Provide clear contact information, privacy policies, and testimonials. Ensure the site is secure (HTTPS).
  • Helpfulness: Focus on answering user questions thoroughly and providing actionable advice, not just selling services.

On-Page Optimization

  • Keyword Strategy: Conduct in-depth keyword research to identify terms patients use (e.g., "treatment-resistant depression therapy," "weight loss injections near me").
  • Title Tags & Meta Descriptions: Optimize these for every page to be compelling, include target keywords, and encourage clicks in search results.
  • Heading Structure (H1, H2, H3): Use headings logically to break up content and incorporate keywords naturally.
  • Image Optimization: Use descriptive `alt` text for all images, especially for accessibility and search engine understanding. Compress images for faster loading.
  • Content Depth: Ensure service pages and blog posts are comprehensive and cover topics in detail.

Off-Page SEO (Backlinks & Authority)

Off-page SEO primarily involves building high-quality backlinks from other reputable websites. These links act as "votes of confidence," signaling to search engines that your site is trustworthy and authoritative within the healthcare niche.

  • Backlink Profile Audit: Analyze the current backlink profile for quality, relevance, and diversity. Disavow any low-quality or spammy links.
  • Content Promotion: Promote your high-quality content (e.g., articles on SPRAVATO, Tirzepatide) to relevant medical blogs, health news sites, and professional organizations.
  • Local Citations: Ensure consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) information across all online directories (Google Business Profile, Yelp, Healthgrades, etc.) for local SEO.
  • Guest Posting: Seek opportunities to contribute expert articles to reputable health and wellness publications.
  • Partnerships: Collaborate with other healthcare providers or related businesses for legitimate link-building opportunities.
